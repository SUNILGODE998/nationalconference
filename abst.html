Abstract Submission

Authors are warmly invited to submit abstracts for their papers, which should present new, unpublished research and must not be submitted to any other conference or journal at the same time. Only accepted and registered papers will be allowed for presentation at the conference. For detailed instructions on how to prepare and submit your abstract and full paper, please visit the conference website.

Publication

After the peer review process, the selected full-length papers based on originality, novelty and suitability will be recommended for possible publication in ANY ONE of the UGC CARE/Scopus/Web of Science/SCI Journals with publication fee.
